What's in the backup?

The ZIP contains six CSV files covering everything in your YieldGuard account:

  • inventory.csv — All inventory items with allergen flags
  • recipes.csv — All recipes with PPDS settings
  • recipe_ingredients.csv — Recipe-to-ingredient links
  • temperature_logs.csv — Full temperature history (incl. voided entries with reasons)
  • cleaning_logs.csv — Cleaning task sign-offs
  • delivery_logs.csv — Goods-in temperature checks
